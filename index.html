How to play

  • 3 rounds of tapping eggs before they disappear
  • Each round gets harder: eggs vanish faster and more appear at once
  • Points per egg increase each round (10, 20, 30)
  • Combo: collect eggs within 1 second for up to a 3x multiplier
  • Each egg gives you a random candy. Try to find all 14!
